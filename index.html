Sudoku Solver

How to Play

  • Fill the grid so that every row, column, and 3×3 box contains the digits 1 to 9.
  • Each number can appear only once in each row, column, and box.
  • Start with the easiest squares — those with the most numbers already filled in.

Rules of Sudoku

  • Use only numbers 1–9.
  • No repeats in any row, column, or 3×3 box.
  • Use logic, not guessing!
“सत्यमेव जयते”
(Truth Alone Triumphs)

Hints & Strategy

  • Scan: Look for rows, columns, or boxes where a number is missing only once.
  • Pencil In: Make small notes of possible numbers in empty cells.
  • Elimination: Use process of elimination to narrow down choices.
  • Stay Calm: Patience and logic are your best friends.
“धैर्यं सर्वत्र साधनम्”
(Patience accomplishes everything)
